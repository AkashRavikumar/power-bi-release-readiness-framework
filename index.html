Power BI Release Readiness Framework

The Power BI Release Readiness Framework is a governance control that ensures Power BI dashboards and semantic models are fit for release before promotion, certification, or production deployment.

High-Level Architecture

    A[Power Apps\nRelease Readiness UI] -->|Custom Connector| B[MCP Server\nRelease Readiness Engine]
    B --> C[Power BI APIs\nMetadata & Admin]
    B --> D[Rule Engine\nPerformance & Governance Checks]
    B --> E[Audit Store\nEvidence & Decisions]

Benefits

  • Consistent and objective release governance
  • Early detection of performance and design risks
  • Reduced manual review effort
  • Clear audit trail for compliance and assurance
